ABOUT NORMAL
CHAOS

Ever want to live spontaneously and NOT pay for what you actually bought? Well billsplitter™ defaults to chaos mode so your bill is automatically split unevenly between your party no matter what settings you chose! It looks normal so you can trick your unattentive friends too (or not). There is also the normal mode if you’re lame. For an even funner one, use the misfortune mode so that one person is randomly chosen to pay most of the bill.
